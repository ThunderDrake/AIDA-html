Discovery

We begin with a strategic online discovery meeting with the company team to delve deep into the project. If necessary, we organize interviews with key stakeholders and client executives. Based on the data we collect from these interviews and our desk research, we prepare a document that includes a survey of the industry market, an analysis of major competitors from a branding perspective, and our initial brand hypothesis.

Deliverables

  • Discovery meeting and interviews with the team to learn about the company's status, upcoming plans, and long-term goals

  • Market research, including trends, perspectives, and barriers

  • Competitor research and analysis

  • Target audience segmentation, tribes, and ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

  • Brand hypothesis

Naming

We start working on your brand name based on the brand platform or a detailed brief. Our team begins by generating a wide range of options before narrowing it down to a presentation of 10 name options that have been preliminary screened for domain and trademark availability. After the approval of 2-3 names, we supervise the final copyright and trademark checks with your legal team. If necessary, we go for a second round of naming.

Deliverables

  • Up to 10 name options in a detailed PDF presentation with descriptions and mood boards

  • Up to 10 additional name options in the second round

  • Up to 10 name options in the third round, if needed, at an additional cost

  • URL recommendations

  • Assistance with the trademark process, if needed, for a separate fee

Logo & Packaging Design Concept

First, we ensure that you provide us with all the assets we need to get started, such as product information, packaging sizes, or dielines from your printer. You will receive a handy checklist of everything needed. We then have a creative meeting with your team to learn all about your brand's visual expectations. At this stage, we research the niche and conduct a strategic design review, including competitor analysis, design trends, color coding, brand architecture schemes, visuals, and more. We discuss the document with you in a meeting to finalize our future visual strategy.

Within 2-3 weeks, we provide you with the first look at your packaging/label design options. We come up with 2-3 concepts on up to 5 SKUs to create a complex vision of the brand architecture. Our team presents the project online and delivers the PDF file with mockups and visualizations. We include up to 10 refinements. After the final approval, the concept is prepared for adaptation.

Deliverables

  • Design review meeting and PDF presentation

  • Up to 10 additional name options in the second round

  • 2-3 packaging/label design concepts, including logos if needed

  • Brand architecture scheme

  • Up to 10 refinements to finalize the concept

Note: The basic cost does not include the following:

  • Original third-party photography, illustration, typography, or stock licensing fees

  • Print or web licensing fees for non-royalty-free fonts

  • Printing, print management, or press-checks

  • Key online profiles to follow for community development

  • Social media templates for ongoing use in vertical and horizontal formats

  • Social media brand identity guide
